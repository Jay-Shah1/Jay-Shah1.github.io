Synthesis of Quantum Dots

With:Satadru Chakrabarty, Prof. Kabeer Jasuja

Performed initial experimentation involving the high shear mixing of an organic solvent, acetonitrile (ACN), inside a kitchen blender. After conducting such an experiment, we found that the appearance of ACN had changed from colorless to brownish yellow. The motivation behind this was to understand how an unwanted interaction between a polymeric material and an organic solvent could result in a dispersion of fluorescent carbon quantum dots.

Smart Sun Visor

With:Souritra Garai

We developed a smart sun visor, a small electronic device that sits on the windshield of a car, shielding drivers from sudden or bright sunlight that can cause temporary blindness, turning them into a road hazard. Our invention actively tracks the sun and positions a small disc of light-filtering material on the brightest area of the windshield, intercepting sunlight before it strikes the driver’s eyes.

Learned to use Arduino and integrate sensors to create custom electronic solutions. Gained experience in 3D printing techniques, enabling rapid prototyping.
